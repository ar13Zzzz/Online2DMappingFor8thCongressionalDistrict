MARAGONDON

Embark on a captivating journey to Maragondon, Cavite, Philippines, and uncover a treasure trove of historical landmarks and natural wonders. From the iconic Bonifacio Trial House, where the Katipunan leader was tried, to the picturesque Pico de Loro mountain, offering breathtaking hiking adventures, Maragondon is a destination that seamlessly blends history and nature, promising an unforgettable experience for all who visit.
